About Us

We are a team of dedicated, caring individuals that want to protect the Earth. Plastic waste is one of the main ways that the environment is negatively impacted, especially marine life. In order to combat this issue, we are selling reusable straws through the Junior Achievement program.

Corporate Social Responsibility

Our goal is to save marine life by reducing the amount of plastic waste in our oceans. Stro donates 10% of total profits to Plastic Oceans Canada, an organization that strives to raise awareness and find a solution to this problem.
